# Sales-Analysis
A report that visualizes the insights from a sales dataset
Hi Everyone

I am thrilled to share my first project with MeriSKILL In the sales dataset given, I have analysed the data to identify sales trends, cities with the highest order and sale, sales trend by month, top selling products for business decision making.
Tool: PowerBI

The following are the insight generated from the data:
- The Total sale: 34.49M
- The Total product sold: 19
- The Total product ordered: 209K
- The no of cities that ordered the products: 9

- San Francisco is the highest generating city for the company

-MacBook pro laptop has the highest revenue of product sold for 8.0M

-Amongst the top 5 product ordered, AAA Batteries (4 pack) emerge as the highest product ordered. While being the highest product ordered, it is observed that it is in the least 5 product that generated a low revenue due to its low item price.
